The LuminaWash Difference

Our 5-Step Cleaning Process.

Every garment goes through our rigorous, hospital-grade cleaning protocol to ensure maximum cleanliness and fabric longevity.

1. Inspect & Tag

Garments are individually inspected, logged, and photographed for tracking.

2. Pre-Treat

Stains are identified and treated with specialized, eco-friendly enzymes.

3. Clean

Washed in purified water using computer-controlled detergent dosing.

4. Finish

Air-dried or delicately pressed based on fabric requirements.

5. Package

Passed through QA, perfectly folded, and sealed in breathable bags.
