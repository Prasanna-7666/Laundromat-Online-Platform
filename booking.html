Quick Guide

How to Prepare Your Laundry

Follow these simple steps for a seamless pickup experience.

Bag It Up

Place your garments in any disposable bag for your first order. We will return them in a complimentary, reusable LuminaWash premium garment bag.

Check Your Pockets

Please ensure all pockets are emptied of pens, receipts, and valuables. Our robotic sorting will do a final check, but your help speeds up the process.

Special Instructions

Have a stubborn stain? Leave a note inside the bag or add it to the 'Special Instructions' when booking, and our specialists will handle it.
